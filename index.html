Welcome to The Swaha Hotel

Conveniently situated in the heart of Batubulan, Gianyar Bali, The Swaha Hotel is literally close from famous local market Sukawati. It’s a 30 minutes drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. The Swaha is a unique hotel with 14 well furnished and well appointed rooms with luxury interiors and cooking facilities. The facilities include a good size pool, kid’s pool. Bar and restaurant, free Wi-Fi, spa, 24 hours reception / room service, conducted tours and more.
